# VelNit Brain Changelog

All notable changes to the VelNit Brain (VRIF, TALK, Writing DNA, Knowledge
Graph) are recorded here, oldest first within each version.

## v1.4 - Knowledge Graph (this release)
- Added `05_Knowledge_Graph.md`: 13 core concepts, relationship chains,
  the 8-question concept schema (fully worked for Loneliness and
  Connection), ontology chains, knowledge sources, content mapping, and
  the AI reasoning model.
- Established this `brain/` folder as the versioned, git-tracked home for
  the four foundational documents, replacing loose files.
- Added the Content Operating System (Phase B) as the first system built
  directly against the Brain - see DECISIONS.md.

## v1.3 - Writing DNA
- Added `04_Writing_DNA.md`: voice traits, writing principles, the
  Emotional Journey arc, the Storytelling Formula, vocabulary lists,
  sentence and research style guidance, the Article Blueprint, Facebook
  DNA, the VelNit Filter, and the Editorial Charter.

## v1.2 - TALK Model
- Added `03_TALK_Model.md`: origin story, theory, the four T/A/L/K
  modules, the Conversation Library, the Relationship Intelligence
  Mapping, AI Integration, Measurement, TALK Principles, and the TALK
  Certification Pathway.

## v1.1 - VRIF
- Added `02_VRIF.md`: the definition of Relationship Intelligence, the
  six pillars, indicators, assessment questions, the RQ Index concept,
  AI-per-pillar mapping, product implications, ethical principles, and
  the future research agenda.

## v1.0 - Foundation
- VelNit Life public marketing site and core Supabase/Netlify
  architecture (Phase 1).
